Department of Computer Science at the University of Ghana: A Gateway to World-Class Education The Department of Computer Science at the University of Ghana (UG) is a hub for world-class computer science education. Offering a range of programs from short courses to PhDs, the department is committed to providing quality education and industry-relevant research

Short Courses UG offers an eight-week intensive certificate course open to senior high school graduates, tertiary students, school graduates, workers, and the general public. These courses cover a variety of topics, including web programming, information security and ethical hacking, computer network fundamentals, and mobile application development

Undergraduate Programmes The department offers two undergraduate degree programmes: Bachelor of Science in Computer Science (Single-Major and Major-Minor) and Bachelor of Science in Information Technology. These programs open the door to sought-after technology careers with a world-class Bachelor of Science in Computer Science degree

MPhil and MSc Programmes UG's MPhil and MSc programmes offer a unique blend of cutting-edge research and industry experience. These programs embrace world-class graduate education, preparing students for advanced roles in the field

PhD Programmes The department's PhD programmes lead the effort to extend the boundaries of knowledge through quality and industry-relevant research. These programs are designed for those who wish to contribute to the field at the highest level

Expert Instructors and Certification The department boasts instructors with years of industry experience, providing students with a unique opportunity to learn from the best Upon completion of the prescribed exam, students earn a certificate of completion from the department

The Department of Computer Science at the University of Ghana is truly a gateway to world-class computer science education. Whether you're a high school graduate looking to learn new skills, an undergraduate student seeking a solid foundation in computer science, or a professional aiming for advanced knowledge, UG's Department of Computer Science has something to offer you
