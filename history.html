Our History

The Department of Computer Science at the University of Ghana has a rich history of providing quality education in the field of computer science. For more than 25 years, the department has offered computing courses for students from various departments

Today, the Department offers two undergraduate degree programmes, Bachelor of Science in Computer Science Single-Major and Major-Minor as well as Bachelor of Science in Information Technology. The department also offers an eight-week intensive certificate course open to senior high school graduates, tertiary students, school graduates, workers, and the general public

Our department is dedicated to providing students with a high-quality education in computer science. We are committed to teaching the fundamental concepts of computer science along with the latest technological advancements
